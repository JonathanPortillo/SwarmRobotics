Abstract

Shape formation by multi-robotic systems has asignificant role in research of swarm robotics. Our researchrelies mainly on using an autonomous decentralized multi-robotic system to achieve shape formation using discrete andcontinuous algorithms. Our study mainly focuses on the runtimes and efficiency of the controllers used in decentralizedshape formation. Our research uses a consensus algorithm asa basis to relate differences in total distance traveled and timeelapsed
